# Healthy (Review)

## Purpose
This file stores ORIGINAL journaling sentences that show
effort, completion, learning, or forward movement.

Healthy does NOT mean perfect.
Healthy means something moved forward.
- When the story starts as a healthy note- It can complete with healthy and/or unhealthy notes 

## Rules for Claude
- Copy sentences exactly as written by the user
- Do NOT rewrite or summarise
- Do NOT add new words

## What belongs here
- Completed actions
- Tried actions
- Helpful behaviors
- Calm or responsible responses

## Example
I finished my assignment slowly.
I attended the meeting even though I was tired.
