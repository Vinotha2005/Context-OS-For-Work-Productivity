# Unhealthy (Review)

## Purpose
This file stores ORIGINAL journaling sentences that show
avoidance, delay, stress, or breakdown.

Unhealthy does NOT mean failure.
It means something is not working.

## Rules for Claude
- Extract exact sentences only
- No judgement
- No summarising

## What belongs here
- Missed actions
- Delays
- Emotional reactions
- Confusion or stress
